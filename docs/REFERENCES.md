# References (2022–2025)

The following works underpin the methodology, experimental practices, and inverse algorithms reflected in this repository. Use them as primary citations in academic writing:

1. Asif, M., et al. (2022). Inverse analysis of mould–casting interfacial heat transfer with coating effects. *Journal of Thermal Analysis and Calorimetry*.
2. Khalifa, D., Mzali, F. (2022). Optimal Experiment Design for the Identification of the IHTC in Sand Casting. *FDMP – Fluid Dynamics & Materials Processing*.
3. Roy, A. D., et al. (2023). Solutions of one-dimensional inverse heat conduction problems: review and advances. *Transactions of the CSME*.
4. Deng, F., et al. (2023). An indirect evaluation method of mold coating thickness via surface roughness. *Journal of Manufacturing Processes*.
5. Dong, S., Shan, Z. (2023). Investigation of the IHTC of ZL101 in freezing sand mold casting. *Preprint*.
6. Dong, S., et al. (2024). IHTC between frozen sand mold and metal: experiments and modeling. *Journal of Materials Science & Technology*.
7. Vergnano, A., et al. (2024). Identification of heat transfer parameters for gravity sand casting. *Machines*.
8. Matsushita, T., et al. (2024). Penetration behavior of molten cast iron into sand molds. *Metallurgical and Materials Transactions*.
9. Optimized Bayesian IHCP framework (2024). Preprint archive.
10. Chen, Y., et al. (2025). Simplified secant-based inverse method for IHTC identification. *International Journal of Heat and Mass Transfer*.
